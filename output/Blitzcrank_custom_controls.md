### Rocket Grab
- **Notation**: 5S1
- **Your Keys**: Press M
- **Usage**: Core move for controlling space. Safe at range. Use to force opponent into grab range. Enhanced version adds shock effect.

### Air Purifier
- **Notation**: 2S1
- **Your Keys**: Hold S + Press M
- **Usage**: SAFEST MOVE (+44 on block!). Perfect anti-air. Despite 119f recovery, massive frame advantage makes it safe. Use against jumpers. Never needs assist.

- **Notation**: 2S2
- **Your Keys**: Hold S + Press M
- **Usage**: UNBLOCKABLE command grab. HIGH RISK - only use with assist cover OR when opponent whiffs big move OR after conditioning. Very punishable on whiff. Enhanced version has armor.
