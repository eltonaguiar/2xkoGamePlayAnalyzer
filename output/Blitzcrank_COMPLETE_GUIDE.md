## ðŸŽ¯ TOP 3 STRATEGIES
================================================================================

### Strategy 1: Zone Control
**Difficulty**: Easy | **Success Rate**: High in neutral game

**Description**: Use Rocket Grab (5S1) from mid-long range to control space and force opponent to approach on your terms.

**Key Moves**: 5S1 (Rocket Grab), 2S1 (Air Purifier)

**How to Execute:**
1. Stay at mid-range (just outside opponent's poke range)
2. Throw out Rocket Grab (M button) repeatedly
3. If opponent jumps, use Air Purifier (Hold S + M)
4. Once Rocket Grab hits, dash forward and start combo
5. Force them to come to you - don't chase

---

### Strategy 2: Anti-Air Dominance
**Difficulty**: Easy to Medium | **Success Rate**: Very High (+44 advantage)

**Description**: Dominate air space with +44 Air Purifier. Never let opponent jump for free.

**Key Moves**: 2S1 (Air Purifier), j.S1 (Air Rocket Grab)

**How to Execute:**
1. Watch for opponent jumps
2. Immediately use Air Purifier (Hold S + M)
3. Even if blocked, you're +44 frames ahead
4. Use frame advantage to mix: low (Hold S+J) or grab (Hold S+M+M)
5. Make them scared to jump - condition them to stay grounded

---

### Strategy 3: 50-50 Mix-up Game
**Difficulty**: Hard (requires reads) | **Success Rate**: Very High at close range

**Description**: Once close, force opponent into guessing situations: block (loses to grab) or jump (loses to hit).

**Key Moves**: 2S2 (Command Grab), 5L (Light Punch), 2L (Low Poke), Assist Call

**How to Execute:**
1. Get close via Rocket Grab or jump-in
2. Call assist (Tag button) for safety
3. Choose: Command Grab (Hold S+M+M) OR Normal Attack (J/K/L)
4. If they block: Grab wins
5. If they jump: Your attack wins
6. Repeat mix-up - they must keep guessing

## âš”ï¸ MATCHUP GUIDE
================================================================================

### Tough Matchups:

#### vs Zoners / Long-Range Characters - Hard

**Why Difficult**: Can keep Blitzcrank out, avoid Rocket Grab, control space better

**Counter-Strategy**: Patient approach, use Air Purifier liberally, don't chase recklessly

**Key Points:**
- Walk forward slowly - don't dash (too committal)
- Block projectiles and gain ground
- Use Air Purifier (Hold S+M) to stuff their pokes
- Once you land one Rocket Grab, they're in your range
- Use assist to cover your approach

**Moves to Use**: 2S1 frequently, Careful forward movement, Assists
**Moves to Avoid**: Random Rocket Grab from far (gets punished), Jumping predictably

---

#### vs Rushdown / Fast Characters - Medium-Hard

**Why Difficult**: Can pressure Blitzcrank, exploit slow buttons, bad defense

**Counter-Strategy**: Keep them out with Rocket Grab, use Air Purifier to reset neutral

**Key Points:**
- DON'T let them get in - Blitzcrank defense is bad
- Use Rocket Grab to create distance when they approach
- Air Purifier (+44) resets pressure to your favor
- Parry (Press U) their predictable pressure
- Once you get your turn, command grab immediately

**Moves to Use**: Rocket Grab for spacing, Air Purifier to reset, Parry
**Moves to Avoid**: Pressing buttons when minus, Challenging their pressure without assist

---

#### vs Characters with Good Backdash - Medium

**Why Difficult**: Can backdash away from command grab 50-50

**Counter-Strategy**: Corner them, use forward-moving attacks, call assist to cover backdash

**Key Points:**
- Push them to corner where backdash is useless
- Use forward-moving attacks (5M, 5H) that catch backdash
- Call assist BEFORE command grab to cover backdash
- Throw (Press I) is faster and catches backdash better
- Mix in forward dash > grab to catch backdash recovery

**Moves to Use**: Assist + Command Grab, Normal Throw, Forward normals
**Moves to Avoid**: Raw command grab without assist

---

### Favorable Matchups:

#### vs Big Body / Slow Characters
- **Advantage**: Rocket Grab is hard for them to avoid, easy to combo
- **How to Exploit**: Zone heavily with Rocket Grab, they can't respond fast enough

#### vs Characters Without Good Anti-Airs
- **Advantage**: Can jump in freely, avoid their neutral game
- **How to Exploit**: Jump-in > combo repeatedly, abuse air mobility

### Universal Tips:

- Always have assist ready before going for command grab
- If they jump a lot, spam Air Purifier - it's +44!
- In corner, they can't escape your command grab mixup
- Never challenge opponent when you're minus - wait your turn
- One Rocket Grab hit often leads to 50% health gone
