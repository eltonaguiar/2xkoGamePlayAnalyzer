## ‚ö° What Does "12f Startup" Mean?

### The Technical Explanation:
**12 frames until the move hits**

### What That ACTUALLY Means:

**Frame = 1/60th of a second** (the game runs at 60 FPS)
- 12 frames = 12/60 second = **0.2 seconds** (one-fifth of a second)

**In Real Time:**
- You press the button (J key)
- 0.2 seconds pass
- The punch becomes active and can hit

### Is 12f Fast or Slow?

**Fighting Game Speed Chart:**
```
3-5f   = SUPER FAST (unreactable)
6-8f   = FAST (hard to react)
9-12f  = MEDIUM (normal speed)
13-20f = SLOW (reactable)
20+f   = VERY SLOW (easy to see coming)
```

**Blitzcrank's 5L at 12f = MEDIUM speed**
- Not super fast, but not slow either
- It's his fastest normal move
- Most characters have 6-8f lights, so Blitz is a bit slower

### Why Does This Matter?

**1. Can you react to opponent's moves?**
If opponent does a 20f move, you can see it coming and press 5L (12f) to hit them first.

**2. Can you punish blocked moves?**
If opponent is -15 on block, you have 15 frames to punish. Your 12f 5L will hit them!

**3. Can you interrupt pressure?**
If there's a gap in opponent's blockstring, 12f might be fast enough to jab out.

## üõ°Ô∏è What Does "-2 On Block" Mean?

### The Technical Explanation:
**You recover 2 frames AFTER the opponent**

### What That ACTUALLY Means:

**When they block your 5L:**
1. Your move hits their block
2. Both of you are in "blockstun" (frozen briefly)
3. You recover after 12f + recovery time
4. They recover 2 frames BEFORE you do
5. **They get to act first!**

### Frame Advantage Chart:

```

  ‚Üì

  ‚Üì

```

## üö® Dealing With Pressure: "I'm Being Locked Down!"

### The Problem:
```
Opponent keeps attacking you:
‚Üí Crouching light (2L) spam
‚Üí Grab mixup (throw or command grab)
‚Üí Assist call to cover gaps
‚Üí You're just blocking forever
‚Üí Eventually you guess wrong and die
```

### Your Options (From Safest to Riskiest):

**1. Just Block (Safest)**
```
What: Hold back/down-back to block everything
When: When you don't know what to do
Why: Blocks all strikes (not grabs)
Risk: They can grab you
Reward: You stay safe

If they grab you: Press I (normal throw) to tech (counter) it
If they command grab: You have to jump or backdash BEFORE it comes out
```

**2. Parry (Medium Risk)**
```
What: Press U right before their attack hits
When: You read they'll attack (not grab)
Why: Parries their attack, gives YOU frame advantage
Risk: If you parry nothing, you're vulnerable
Reward: You get to attack first, turn the tables

Timing: Press U 6-10 frames before their hit
Success: Blue flash, you're now plus, press 5L to start YOUR turn
Failure: Parry animation whiffs, they hit you
```

**3. Mash Your Fastest Move (High Risk)**
```
What: Press 5L (12f) during gaps in their pressure
When: You see a gap >12 frames
Why: Interrupts their offense
Risk: If no gap exists, you get counter-hit
Reward: You interrupt, reset to neutral or start YOUR combo

How to find gaps:
- After they do 5L on block (-2), if they do another 5L (12f startup), there's a 14f gap
- After assists hit (usually +frames), there's NO gap
- After special moves on block, check frame data for gaps
```

**4. Reversal Special Move (Very High Risk)**
```
What: Do invincible move (super, if you have one)
When: Under extreme pressure, about to die
Why: Invincible moves go through attacks
Risk: If blocked, you're -20 or worse (full combo punish)
Reward: You escape pressure, might even hit them

Blitzcrank has NO true reversal ‚ùå
- No invincible moves
- MUST use options 1-3 above
```

**5. Backdash (Medium Risk)**
```
What: Double-tap back (A A) to dash backward
When: They're in your face but not attacking yet
Why: Creates space, resets to neutral
Risk: Backdash has recovery frames, can be hit
Reward: Get out of corner, reset to your range (mid-range for Rocket Grab)

Good: After blocking their unsafe move
Bad: During their blockstring (you'll get hit)
```

**6. Jump (High Risk)**
```
What: Press W to jump backward or neutral
When: You read they'll do a grab or low attack
Why: Avoids grabs (can't grab airborne)
Risk: They anti-air you (2S1 or similar)
Reward: Escape grab mixup, might land a jump-in

Good: Against command grab attempts
Bad: Against good players who anti-air on reaction
```

## üéÆ Mirror Match Gameplan (Blitzcrank vs Blitzcrank)

### Understanding the Mirror:
```
Both players have:
- Same health (1050)
- Same moves
- Same weaknesses
- Command grab (2S2) beats blocking
- Rocket Grab (5S1) controls space
- Air Purifier (2S1) +44 advantage
```

### Winning Strategy:

**Phase 1: Neutral Game (No One Touching)**

**Goal:** Hit Rocket Grab first, or get Air Purifier advantage

```
What to do:
1. Stay at mid-range
2. Throw Rocket Grab (5S1) repeatedly
3. If they jump ‚Üí Air Purifier (2S1)
4. If your Rocket Grab hits ‚Üí Dash forward, start combo
5. If they Rocket Grab you ‚Üí You're pulled in, they get combo
6. Call assist to cover your Rocket Grabs

Key Rules:
- DON'T jump predictably (they'll Air Purifier you)
- DON'T walk forward carelessly (Rocket Grab pulls you)
- DO spam Air Purifier if they jump (you get +44!)
- DO use assists to make YOUR moves safe

Who wins neutral: Player who lands Rocket Grab or Air Purifier first
```

**Phase 2: Your Turn (You're Plus)**

**Scenario:** You landed Air Purifier (+44) or punished their whiff

```
What to do:
1. Walk up (they can't challenge, you're plus)
2. Call assist (Tag button)
3. Now do command grab mixup:

Option A: Command Grab (2S2)
- If they block: GRAB WINS! ‚úÖ
- If they jump: Grab whiffs, you're punished ‚ùå

Option B: 5L or 2L
- If they jump: ATTACK WINS! ‚úÖ
- If they block: You're -2, but assist covers you

This is 50-50 mixup:
- They must guess: block or jump
- If they guess wrong: You win
- If they guess right: Reset to neutral
```

**Phase 3: Defense (They're Pressuring You)**

**Scenario:** They landed Air Purifier or Rocket Grab on you

```
What to do (in priority order):

1. BLOCK EVERYTHING (safest)
   - Hold back/down-back
   - Wait for them to do command grab

2. WHEN THEY CALL ASSIST + COMMAND GRAB:
   - Jump BEFORE grab comes out (6f)
   - Or backdash BEFORE grab
   - DON'T press buttons (grab will get you)

3. IF THEY DON'T HAVE ASSIST (on cooldown):
   - Command grab without assist = punishable
   - You can press 2L (10f) to interrupt
   - Or jump on reaction

4. PARRY THEIR NORMALS (risky)
   - If they press 5L, parry it (U)
   - You get frame advantage back
   - Loses to their command grab
```

**Phase 4: Assist Cooldowns (CRITICAL)**

**Assist cooldown = ~8-10 seconds** after use

```
If YOUR assist is on cooldown:
‚ùå Don't do command grab (too risky without assist)
‚úÖ Use Rocket Grab to keep distance
‚úÖ Use Air Purifier for frame advantage
‚úÖ Play defensive until assist returns

If THEIR assist is on cooldown:
‚úÖ Push forward aggressively
‚úÖ Their command grab is unsafe (press 2L to beat it)
‚úÖ They can't make unsafe moves safe
‚úÖ This is YOUR window to attack!

How to track:
- Look at assist icon (grayed out = on cooldown)
- Count ~8 seconds after they use it
- Attack when you see it's unavailable
```
