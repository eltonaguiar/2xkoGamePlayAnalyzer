## üî• MOST IMPORTANT MOVES

### Move 1: Air Purifier (Your Safest Move!)
- **Notation**: 2S1
- **Keyboard**: Hold **S** (Down) + Press **M** (Special)
- **English**: Crouching Special / Diagonal uppercut grab
- **What it looks like**: Blitzcrank shoots his fist diagonally upward
- **On Block**: +44 frames (SUPER SAFE!)
- **When to use**: When opponent jumps at you

---

### Move 2: Rocket Grab (Your Main Move!)
- **Notation**: 5S1
- **Keyboard**: Just Press **M** (Special) while standing
- **English**: Standing Special / Forward rocket punch
- **What it looks like**: Blitzcrank shoots his fist forward like a rocket
- **On Block**: Safe at long range
- **When to use**: From far away to pull opponents close

---

### Move 3: Command Grab (Win Condition!)
- **Notation**: 2S2
- **Keyboard**: Hold **S** (Down) + Press **M** twice quickly
- **English**: Down Special 2 / Close grab
- **What it looks like**: Blitzcrank grabs them close
- **CANNOT BE BLOCKED!**
- **‚ö†Ô∏è RISKY**: Only use with assist or when opponent messes up
- **When to use**: Close range, when they're blocking too much

## üí• FRAME DATA EXPLAINED SIMPLY

### What Are Frames?
The game runs at 60 frames per second. Each "frame" is 1/60th of a second.

### On Block Frame Data:
- **+44 (like Air Purifier)**: You recover 44 frames BEFORE them = Super safe!
- **+1 to +9**: You recover first = Safe, your turn
- **0**: Both recover same time = Neutral
- **-2 (like Light Punch)**: They recover 2 frames before you = Slightly risky
- **-5 or worse**: They recover way before you = DANGER! They can punish!
- **-15 (like blocked 5S2)**: They get 15 frames to punish you = VERY BAD!

## üî¥ PUNISH GUIDE (Simple!)

### If Opponent Blocks Your 5S2 (Rocket Punch): -15f
**You're in BIG trouble! Opponent can:**
- Press **J** (Light) then do full combo
- Hold **S+J** (Crouch Light) then do full combo
- Pretty much anything they want!

**What to do**: Don't use 5S2 without thinking! Make sure it hits!

---

### If They Block Your Light Punch: -2f
**You're slightly unsafe:**
- They have a tiny window to hit you
- You can still try to block or move away
- Best option: Cancel into special move (Press **M** right after)

**What to do**: After **J** (Light), quickly press **M** (Rocket Grab) to make it safer!

---

### If They Block Your Air Purifier: +44f
**YOU'RE SUPER SAFE!**
- You can do pretty much anything!
- They have to block or get hit
- Time to pressure them!

**What to do**: After they block, immediately:
- Press **J** (Light) for combo
- Hold **S+M+M** (Command Grab) for mix-up
- Hold **S+J** (Crouch Light) for low attack

## üéÆ EASY BNB COMBOS (Keyboard Version)

### Combo 1: Light Chain (Easiest!)
**Notation**: 5L > 5L > 5S1

**Keyboard**:
1. Press **J** (Light)
2. Press **J** (Light) again
3. Press **M** (Rocket Grab)

**English**: Jab, jab, Rocket Grab

**When to use**: After any light hit lands

---

### Combo 2: Jump-In Combo (Most Common!)
**Notation**: j.M > 5L > 5M > 5H > 5S1

**Keyboard**:
1. Press **D+W** (Forward Jump) then **K** (Medium) in air
2. Land, then Press **J** (Light)
3. Press **K** (Medium)
4. Press **L** (Heavy)
5. Press **M** (Rocket Grab)

**English**: Jump forward medium, jab, medium punch, heavy punch, Rocket Grab

**When to use**: After you land a jump-in attack

---

### Combo 3: Rocket Grab Follow-Up (Key Combo!)
**Notation**: 5S1 (hit) > dash > 5L > 5M > 2S1

**Keyboard**:
1. Press **M** (Rocket Grab) - it hits!
2. Tap **D+D** quickly (Dash forward)
3. Press **J** (Light)
4. Press **K** (Medium)
5. Hold **S+M** (Air Purifier)

**English**: Rocket Grab hits, dash, jab, medium, Air Purifier

**When to use**: After Rocket Grab pulls them in

**Pro Tip**: Ends with +44, so you're super safe!

---

### Combo 4: Low Starter
**Notation**: 2L > 2M > 5S1 > dash > 5L > 5H

**Keyboard**:
1. Hold **S+J** (Crouch Light)
2. Hold **S+K** (Crouch Medium)
3. Release **S**, Press **M** (Rocket Grab)
4. Tap **D+D** (Dash)
5. Press **J** (Light)
6. Press **L** (Heavy)

**English**: Low jab, low medium, Rocket Grab, dash, jab, heavy

**When to use**: Mix-up with low attacks

---

### Combo 5: With Assist (Max Damage!)
**Notation**: 5L > 5M > [Assist] > 5H > 5S1 > dash > 2S1

**Keyboard**:
1. Press **J** (Light)
2. Press **K** (Medium)
3. Press **[Tag Button]** (Call assist)
4. While assist hits, Press **L** (Heavy)
5. Press **M** (Rocket Grab)
6. Tap **D+D** (Dash)
7. Hold **S+M** (Air Purifier)

**English**: Jab, medium, assist, heavy, Rocket Grab, dash, Air Purifier

**When to use**: When you want maximum damage

---

### Combo 6: Command Grab Setup (Win Condition!)
**Notation**: 5L > 5M > [Assist] > 2S2 OR 5L

**Keyboard**:
1. Press **J** (Light)
2. Press **K** (Medium)
3. Press **[Tag Button]** (Assist)
4. **NOW CHOOSE:**
   - Hold **S+M+M** (Command Grab) - They can't block!
   - OR Press **J** (Light) - If they jump

**English**: Jab, medium, assist, then grab OR hit

**When to use**: 50-50 mix-up - they must guess!

**This is how you win!** They have to guess whether to:
- **Block** (loses to Command Grab)
- **Jump** (loses to your Light attack)

## üéØ PUNISH OPPORTUNITIES (Keyboard Version)

### When Opponent Whiffs Big Move
**They miss a slow attack!**

**You do**:
- Hold **S+M+M** (Command Grab) - FREE GRAB!
- OR Press **M** (Rocket Grab) then combo
- OR Press **D+W** then **L** (Jump Heavy) for full combo

---

### When Opponent Jumps
**They're in the air!**

**You do**:
- Hold **S+M** (Air Purifier) - +44 safe!
- OR Press **W** then **M** in air (Air Rocket Grab)

---

### When Opponent Blocks Too Much
**They're just holding block!**

**You do**:
- Press **I** (Normal Throw) - Quick grab
- OR Hold **S+M+M** (Command Grab) + Call Assist
- Remember: Grabs CANNOT be blocked!

---

### When Opponent Very Minus (Blocked unsafe move)
**They're stuck after a bad move!**

**You do**:
- Press **J** (Light) then full combo
- OR Hold **S+M+M** (Command Grab) if close
- OR Press **[Super Button]** if you have meter

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES (Keyboard Version)

### ‚ùå DON'T DO THIS:
1. **Spam Hold S+M+M** (Command Grab) without setup
   - They'll just jump and punish you!
   
2. **Press M+M while far away** (Special 2)
   - Gets blocked = -15 frames = You get punished!

3. **Forget to Hold S+M** (Air Purifier) vs jumpers
   - It's +44! Use it!

4. **Press buttons when you're minus**
   - If they block your unsafe move, DON'T press more buttons!

5. **Use big moves without calling assist**
   - Too risky alone!

---

### ‚úÖ DO THIS:
1. **Press M often** (Rocket Grab from far away)
   - Controls space, pulls them close

2. **Call assist BEFORE Hold S+M+M** (Command Grab)
   - Makes the grab safe if they jump

3. **Hold S+M vs jumpers** (Air Purifier)
   - Pulls them down, +44 advantage!

4. **Press J then M** (Light into Rocket Grab)
   - Makes your light punch safer

5. **Watch for -15 situations**
   - If they block your M+M, you're in trouble!
