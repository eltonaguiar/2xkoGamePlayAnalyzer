## üìñ How to Read This Guide (For Beginners)

### Understanding Move Notation

Fighting games use **numpad notation** for directions (like a keyboard numpad):

```
7  8  9     [Back-Up]  [Up]      [Forward-Up]
4  5  6     [Back]     [Neutral] [Forward]
1  2  3     [Back-Down][Down]    [Forward-Down]
```

### What the Numbers and Letters Mean:
- **5** = Standing (no direction held)
- **2** = Crouching (holding down)
- **6** = Moving forward
- **j.** = Jumping (in the air)

### Button Names:
- **L** = Light attack (fast, low damage)
- **M** = Medium attack (balanced speed and damage)
- **H** = Heavy attack (slow, high damage)
- **S1** = Special move 1
- **S2** = Special move 2

### Examples:
- **5L** = Standing Light Punch (just press Light while standing)
- **2M** = Crouching Medium Kick (hold Down + press Medium)
- **j.H** = Jumping Heavy Attack (press Heavy while in the air)
- **5S1** = Standing Special 1 (Blitzcrank's Rocket Grab)

### Frame Data Explained Simply:
- **Startup**: How long before the move hits (lower is faster)
- **Recovery**: How long you're stuck after the move (lower is safer)
- **On Block**: What happens if opponent blocks
  - **Positive (+)**: You recover first = safe, you can attack again
  - **Negative (-)**: They recover first = unsafe, they can punish you
  - **Example**: +44 means you're SUPER safe! -2 means slightly unsafe

## Frame Advantage Quick Guide

### What Frame Advantage Means:
- **Positive (+)**: You recover first, you have advantage
- **Negative (-)**: Opponent recovers first, they have advantage
- **0**: Both recover at the same time (neutral)

### Frame Advantage Categories:
- **+10 or more**: HUGE advantage - guaranteed pressure or mixup
- **+1 to +9**: Good advantage - your turn to attack
- **0 to -2**: Slightly unsafe - can contest or retreat
- **-3 to -5**: Unsafe - opponent can attack
- **-6 or worse**: VERY UNSAFE - opponent gets full punish

### Blitzcrank's Frame Situations:
- **2S1 on block (+44)**: YOU GET FREE MIXUP! üéâ
- **5L on block (-2)**: Slightly unsafe, space it or cancel it
